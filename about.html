About

Operator turned builder. After 19+ years running businesses, I'm now building civic tech tools to make government work better for citizens and small businesses.

The Operator Years

I spent nearly two decades as a COO and CEO, building and scaling real businesses with real constraints. I've led services-heavy organizations through growth, complexity, and exit.

19+ Years Operating
$13M→$44M Revenue Scaled

I built operating playbooks across sales, operations, finance, and delivery. I designed systems that removed friction, increased throughput, and created accountability. Then I helped exit to a PE-backed strategic acquirer.

The Shift

Now I'm applying that same operational mindset to civic problems. I'm not building apps to demo well — I'm building tools that expose how systems actually work and where they break down.

My current focus is GreenLights Civic Tech: a series of transparency tools starting in NYC and expanding outward. The goal is simple — make it easier for citizens and small businesses to see what's really happening with their government.

Current Focus

  • Geographic scope: Huntington, NY → NYC → New York State → US
  • Problem space: Government transparency and efficiency
  • Approach: Build prototypes fast, learn from real data, iterate
  • Philosophy: Apply AI where it compounds leverage, not where it demos well

Background

Originally from Hicksville, New York. B.S. in Finance from the University of Maryland, College Park. Before the CEO/COO track, I worked in real estate finance, asset management, and portfolio administration.
